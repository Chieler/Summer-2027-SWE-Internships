# 🏆 Most Influential Tech Companies

The curated list of companies whose **2027 internships** are highlighted in a
dedicated section at the top of [`README.md`](README.md).

It combines two well-known reference sets:

1. **[TIME100 Most Influential Companies 2025](https://time.com/collections/time100-companies-2025/)**
   — the technology / AI / software / internet subset (e.g. Meta, Amazon,
   Netflix, Coinbase, Nvidia, OpenAI, Anthropic, Anduril, SpaceX, Scale AI,
   Alibaba, Nintendo, Waymo, LinkedIn, Coursera, Substack, DeepL, DeepSeek).
2. **[Largest technology companies by market cap (2026)](https://companiesmarketcap.com/tech/largest-tech-companies-by-market-cap/)**
   — Nvidia, Apple, Microsoft, Alphabet/Google, Amazon, Meta, Broadcom, Tesla,
   TSMC, Oracle, and the rest of the mega-cap tech field.

Rounded out with the most prominent private AI labs, fintech/quant-trading
firms, developer-infrastructure companies, and semiconductor / space / defense
tech that consistently run large SWE internship programs.

> The `search.py` tracker matches these against every role it pulls (by
> normalized company name) and surfaces the hits as
> **"🏆 Most Influential Tech Companies — 2027 Internships"** at the top of the
> README. Matching is whole-word, so `Amazon`, `Amazon Web Services`, and
> `Amazon.com` all resolve to the same company.
